The Impact of Technology on Youth Mental Health

Question 1 of 15
Comprehension
What is the primary concern about social media use among teenagers?
Explanation

Research shows that excessive social media use is correlated with increased rates of anxiety, depression, and poor sleep quality in teenagers. The constant comparison and validation-seeking behavior can negatively impact mental well-being.

Question 2 of 15
Vocabulary
What does "FOMO" stand for?
Explanation

FOMO (Fear of Missing Out) is a psychological phenomenon where people experience anxiety about not being included in social events or experiences. Social media amplifies FOMO by constantly showing curated highlights of others' lives.

Question 3 of 15
Grammar: Passive Voice with Reporting Verbs
Which sentence correctly uses passive voice?
Explanation

The correct structure is "It is estimated that + clause." This passive construction with reporting verbs is used in academic writing to present statistics or claims in an objective manner.

Question 4 of 15
Grammar: Passive Voice with Reporting Verbs
Complete: "Social media is argued to ___"
Explanation

With "is argued to," use the infinitive form: "have negative effects..." This passive voice structure presents claims or arguments in an academic, distanced manner. The infinitive maintains grammatical correctness.

Question 5 of 15
Comprehension
How does the "dopamine feedback loop" relate to social media addiction?
Explanation

The dopamine feedback loop is a neurochemical mechanism where social media notifications (likes, comments, shares) trigger dopamine release in the brain. This creates a reward cycle similar to gambling, encouraging compulsive use and potentially leading to addiction.

Question 6 of 15
Vocabulary
What does "cyberbullying" mean?
Explanation

Cyberbullying involves using digital platforms to harass, threaten, or humiliate others. Unlike traditional bullying, it can be anonymous, permanent, and reach a wider audience, making it particularly harmful to young people's mental health.

Question 7 of 15
Grammar: Passive Voice with Reporting Verbs
Which sentence is grammatically correct?
Explanation

Both structures are correct. "It is reported that..." and "Excessive screen time is reported to..." are both valid passive voice constructions. They present information in an objective, academic manner.

Question 8 of 15
Critical Thinking
Can technology be both harmful and beneficial for youth mental health?
Explanation

Technology is a double-edged sword. While it enables connection, support groups, and access to mental health resources, it also facilitates cyberbullying, addiction, sleep disruption, and social comparison. The impact depends on how it's used.

Question 9 of 15
Vocabulary
What does "digital detox" mean?
Explanation

A digital detox is a deliberate break from technology use. It can help reset dopamine levels, improve sleep quality, reduce anxiety, and encourage face-to-face social interaction. Many mental health professionals recommend periodic digital detoxes for teenagers.

Question 10 of 15
Grammar: Passive Voice with Reporting Verbs
Complete: "Teenagers are believed to ___"
Explanation

With "are believed to," use the base infinitive: "spend." This passive voice structure presents beliefs or claims about behavior in an objective manner. The infinitive form is grammatically required after the auxiliary verb.

Question 11 of 15
Comprehension
What is the relationship between social media and body image issues?
Explanation

Social media's curated, filtered content creates unrealistic beauty standards. Constant exposure to idealized images leads to social comparison, body dissatisfaction, and increased risk of eating disorders and low self-esteem in both boys and girls.

Question 12 of 15
Vocabulary
What does "mental health literacy" mean?
Explanation

Mental health literacy is the knowledge and understanding of mental health conditions, their symptoms, risk factors, and available treatments. Improving mental health literacy among youth can help reduce stigma and encourage help-seeking behavior.

Question 13 of 15
Grammar: Passive Voice with Reporting Verbs
Which sentence correctly uses passive voice?
Explanation

The correct structure is "It is suggested that + clause." This passive construction is used in academic writing to present recommendations or expert advice without directly attributing them to a specific source.

Question 14 of 15
Critical Thinking
What strategies could help teenagers use technology more mindfully?
Explanation

Mindful technology use involves practical strategies like setting daily screen time limits, using app blockers, scheduling device-free times, practicing digital detoxes, and developing awareness of how social media affects emotions. Education and self-awareness are key.
